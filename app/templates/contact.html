Frequently Asked Questions

What is DirectFarm?

DirectFarm is an online marketplace connecting local farmers with consumers, offering fresh, sustainable, and locally-grown produce.

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We’ll deliver fresh produce directly to your doorstep.

Is there a minimum order amount?

No, you can order as much or as little as you like, though delivery fees may apply for small orders.

How do I become a registered farmer?

Click on the “Sign Up Now” button on the homepage and fill out the registration form. Our team will review and get in touch with you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and other popular payment methods.
